Why Cybertect Exists

Online Waste Problem

Digital advertising doesn’t just lose money to bots—it loses money to bad data. When impressions can’t be verified, analytics get inflated, or tags fire twice, your dashboards stay “green” while ROI quietly evaporates.

Where online waste hides

  • Invisible impressions: ads served but never truly viewed.
  • Broken measurement: attribution gaps and tracking failures masking real performance.
  • Inflated analytics: GA4 sessions and events that don’t reconcile with delivery.
  • Duplicate tags & events: multiple pixels firing from shared templates.
  • Mismatch across systems: platform reports vs analytics behavior that doesn’t add up.

Why most tools miss it

Most fraud tools chase bots. Cybertect follows the numbers—reconciling what was served, what was measured, and what actually happened across ad delivery and analytics.

What Cybertect is built to do

We detect measurement waste and integrity issues before you keep paying for them by flagging discrepancies, surfacing duplicated tracking, and producing evidence packs you can act on.
